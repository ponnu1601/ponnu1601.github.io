A mathematician who fell in love with the stories data tells.

Originally from Kollam, Kerala, India — I came to the US for my master's and fell in love with the intersection of rigorous mathematics and real-world problem solving. My background in pure mathematics gives me a stronger foundation than most for statistical reasoning, while my MS in Data Science from Seattle University sharpened my applied skills across machine learning, analytics, and business intelligence.

I care deeply about making data understandable to decision-makers, not just other data people. Every project I build ends with one question: "So what do we do with this?" That question shapes everything — from feature engineering choices to how I design a dashboard. The goal is never just a model. It's a decision someone can act on.

Currently based in Hillsboro, Oregon, open to Data Scientist, Data Analyst, and Business Intelligence Analyst roles across the US.
